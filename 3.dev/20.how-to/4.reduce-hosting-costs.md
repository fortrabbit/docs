## Use the costs overview

With our dashboard under each of your [payment methods](/1.platform/10.objects/09.payment-method.md) you can find an invoice archive. There is also an invoice draft for the currently ongoing costs for the month so far. The [pro-rated daily billing](/1.platform/12.billing/02.postpaid.md) helps you to remove unnecessary [components](/1.platform/03.concepts/02.components.md) with immediate effect.

## Review apps and environments

Delete! Make sure you have an up-to-date and complete local backup. That way, you can easily re-deploy it again at a later stage.

## Check component plans

Review your currently selected [components](/1.platform/03.concepts/02.components.md). Compare with visitors. You can experiment with a lower scaling of PHP and MySQL. Keep an eye on PHP requests and performance.

For many cases, performance optimized code will require less hosting resource while serving websites fast still. See [performance section](/3.dev/02.performance/).
