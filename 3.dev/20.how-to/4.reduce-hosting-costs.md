## Use the costs overview

In the dashboard, under each of your [payment methods](/1.platform/10.objects/09.payment-method.md), you'll find an invoice archive and a draft invoice for the current month's costs. The [pro-rated daily billing](/1.platform/12.billing/02.postpaid.md) lets you remove unnecessary [components](/1.platform/03.concepts/03.components.md) with immediate effect.

## Review apps and environments

See if you have any unnecessary [apps](/1.platform/10.objects/01.app.md) or [environments](/1.platform/10.objects/02.environment.md) hanging around — maybe some out-of-date side project, or something half finished? Delete! Make sure you have an up-to-date and complete local backup. That way, you can easily re-deploy it again at a later stage.

## Check component plans

Review your currently selected [components](/1.platform/03.concepts/03.components.md). Compare with visitors. You can experiment with a lower scaling of PHP and MySQL. Keep an eye on PHP requests and performance.

## Refactor code

Performance-optimized code often requires fewer hosting resources while still serving websites fast. See the [performance section](/3.dev/02.performance/index.md) for optimization strategies.
