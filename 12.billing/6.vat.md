We consider our primary customers to be entrepreneurs and other businesses. In other words, fortrabbit is primarily a B2B service. This carries some fiscal implications:

* Our prices are shown as net prices without Value Added Tax (VAT)
* If you are a non-VAT registered company in a EU country, we add VAT
* If you are a VAT-registered company in a EU country and add your valid VATIN, we don't charge you VAT
* If you are a VAT-registered company in Germany, we add VAT, but you will get it back from your tax-office
* If you are a company from a NON-EU country, we don't charge you VAT

## EU VAT exemption

We charge no VAT, if article 151(1)(a) VAT Directive applies, e.g. for international bodies, embassies or other EU organisations with a valid VAT exemption certificate. Please get in touch if VAT exemption applies to your organisation.
