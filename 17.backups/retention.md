The [backup component](/9.components/5.backups.md) is available in different plans. Larger ones include more backups. What kinda backups can be expected when?

## Backup retention

Backup retention determines how long backups are kept. This concept is crucial for ensuring data availability, compliance, and efficient storage management.

- Daily backups kept for a short period
- Weekly backups retained for a longer period
- Monthly backups kept for an extended period
