## Why new?

fortrabbit provides hosting for almost two decades. The current fortrabbit platform was established in 2013.

The new platform version of our hosting platform, developed over several years. Everything was redesigned and rebuilt, delivering an enhanced yet intuitive experience. This is our most advanced developer-centric PHP hosting solution, incorporating GitHub integration for automated deployments and streamlined multi-environment staging.

- [Why rewrite?](https://blog.fortrabbit.com/yes-we-rewrite) - blog post on motivation
