We provide a range of available payment types that can be managed with the dashboard. Availability may depend on your geographical location. Beside all classic credit cards, direct bank debit and wallets are supported. SEPA direct debit is accessible to clients in the European Union only.

## Data sharing

Billing data entered will directly be send to our payment provider Stripe.
