The threshold value may change over time and may differ depending on currency and [payment type](/1.platform/12.billing/10.payment-types.md). The minimum amount is typically around 50 cents. fortrabbit uses Stripe as the invoicing provider and their threshold functionality. [Invoices](/1.platform/10.objects/07.invoice.md) below the threshold are nullified automatically, so no payment is due for that service period. The amount carries forward to the payment method's balance and is applied to the next invoice.

## Example

```raw
€0.30 Invoice March -> Threshold applied
€4.50 Invoice April - Apply threshold from balance
€4.80 Amount due in April
```
