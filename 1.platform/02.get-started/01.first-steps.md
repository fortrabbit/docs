fortrabbit is a platform to deploy and host PHP websites and web applications. The [GitHub integration](/1.platform/05.deployment/02.github-app.md) provides deployment directly from the repo. Projects are represented as [apps](/1.platform/10.objects/01.app.md) with [environments](/1.platform/10.objects/02.environment.md). The [collaboration features](/1.platform/20.collaboration/1.intro.md) map real world relations between developers and stakeholders.

### 1. Sign up

Create a free personal account over at our dashboard.

### 2. Create a free trial app

During boarding you will be guided to create your first app. Choose the [free trial](/1.platform/02.get-started/02.free-trial.md) for a quick test drive.

### 3. Explore

Explore all the settings in the dashboard. Login by [SSH](/1.platform/04.code-access/03.ssh.md) or [SFTP](/1.platform/04.code-access/04.sftp.md). [Deploy](/1.platform/05.deployment/01.intro.md) some code. Browse our docs here. There are extensive guides for:

- [Craft CMS](/2.guides/3.craft-cms/01.install.md)
- [Laravel](/2.guides/2.laravel/01.install.md)
- [Kirby CMS](/2.guides/4.kirby/01.intro.md)
- [Statamic](/2.guides/5.statamic/01.install.md)
- [October CMS](/2.guides/7.more/06.october-cms.md)
- and even [WordPress](/2.guides/6.wordpress/01.setup.md)

Please :ContactUs if you have a question or want to leave feedback.

### 4. Start for real

If you like what you see, [migrate existing](/3.dev/20.how-to/migrate-to-fortrabbit.md) or start new projects here. Invite other developers or your clients to [collaborate](/1.platform/20.collaboration/1.intro.md) here.
