Most of what you will configure on fortrabbit lives under the environment settings: the [PHP version and ini values](/1.platform/06.settings/06.php.md), [environment variables](/1.platform/06.settings/01.env-vars.md), the [root path](/1.platform/06.settings/12.root-path.md) for your app, [routing](/1.platform/06.settings/05.routing.md) rules, [password protection](/1.platform/06.settings/03.password-protection.md), and the [incoming](/1.platform/06.settings/20.firewall-incoming.md) and [outgoing](/1.platform/06.settings/21.firewall-outgoing.md) firewalls.

Each environment has its own settings, so production, staging, and feature branches can diverge without breaking each other. The pages below cover what each setting does and when to change it.
