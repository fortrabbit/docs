## Wildcard domains

Wildcard domains are currently not supported at fortrabbit. Please file a support ticket to describe your use case.

## Configuring a domain for e-mail

To receive and send e-mails from your domain you will configure the MX record of your domain with your [domain provider](/1.platform/15.integrations/10.domain-providers.md). Please see your e-mail hosting provider for instructions, here is [more on e-mail hosting from us](/1.platform/15.integrations/12.email-providers.md).
