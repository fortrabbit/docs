## Unlimited

This is the standard role and is often suitable for all members small teams or senior developers of large teams. It gives the person with the role full access on the team, its apps, its members and billing.

- create, delete, configure & scale all apps with the team
- create and delete environments
- access code of all apps of the team
- subscribe and unsubscribe from apps
- delete, create, change payment methods connected to the team
- manage all other member roles
- [invite new members](/1.platform/20.collaboration/19.how-to/01.invite-to-team.md) for any role to the team
- leave the team (if other members are present)
- delete the team

## Limited

Use this role to limit access for junior developers or developers who are not part of the core team.

- delete, configure & scale chosen apps of the team
- create and delete environments
- unsubscribe from team apps
- request access on team apps
- access code on chosen apps
- leave the team
