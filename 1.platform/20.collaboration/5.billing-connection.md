```plain
# Billing connection
person → payment method → app
person → team → payment method → app
```

## What you can do with a billing connection

A billing connection grants permissions to manage the app's team and developers, create or delete apps, and update payment settings.

- Add/remove developers and teams
- Delete apps
- Create apps
- Change the [payment method](/1.platform/10.objects/09.payment-method.md)

## Who can have a billing connection?

Billing connections are available to developers and clients within the fortrabbit platform, with account type determining specific access levels.

- Developers and clients, see [account types](/1.platform/20.collaboration/7.account-types.md)

## Billing connection types

A billing connection can be established either directly or through a team.

### Direct billing connection

A person can connect directly to an app via a personal payment method, which can be shared with others. Developers can also be invited to or invite other developers to connect directly to an app they have access to.

### Team billing connections

Team members with unlimited access—see [team roles](/1.platform/20.collaboration/8.team-roles.md)—have access to all payment methods connected to the team.

## Relation to developer connection

A similar concept is the [developer connection](/1.platform/20.collaboration/6.developer-connection.md), which defines how a person has technical access to an app.
