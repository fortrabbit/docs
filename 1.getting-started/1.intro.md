fortrabbit is a modern web hosting service designed to deliver websites and web applications. It's specifically with Craft CMS, Laravel, Statamic, October CMS and WordPress in mind.

The GitHub integration provides deployment directly from your repository. Your projects are represented as apps, optionally with multiple environments. 

Our collaboration features map real world relations between developers and stakeholders.

fortrabbit is a real company. We are self funded and haver a long time commitment.
