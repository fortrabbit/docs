fortrabbit is a generic PHP host. Most apps you deploy fall into one of a handful of frameworks or content management systems, and each one has its own quirks around environment configuration, persistent storage, deployment hooks, and asset compilation.

The guides below walk through the framework-specific bits for Laravel, Craft CMS, Statamic, Kirby, WordPress, and a few others. Open the guide that matches your stack first, then come back to the platform docs for anything not covered there.
