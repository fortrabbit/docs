## Purpose

The new fortrabbit platform is currently in development. Early adopters with a taste for adventure are welcome to test it.

## Pricing

The platform is free to use during the alpha phase. Prices are displayed for reference and are subject to change.

## Applying

We are gradually onboarding new users. If you are interested, please :ContactUs.

## Terms

The service is provided "as is" during this initial phase of real-world testing. It may contain significant errors, making it unstable and not suitable for production use.

## Migration Path

New releases will be deployed periodically. The next phase is the beta, which will be more feature-complete and paid. Alpha testers will automatically transition to beta testers. The start of the beta phase will be announced upfront via email.

## Limits

- Co-worker invitations are not available
- Only one data center is available
- Metrics are not yet implemented
- Jobs are not yet implemented

## Feedback

We are curious what you think. Please :ContactUs to report bugs or share your ideas.
