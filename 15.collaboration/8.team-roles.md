## Get started

Check out the [teams article](/10.objects/4.team.md) to learn how to create and use teams with the dashboard.

## Unlimited

This is the standard role and is often suitable for all members small teams. It gives the person with thr role full access on the team, it's apps, it's members and billing.

- create, delete, configure & scale all apps with the tam
- access code of all apps of the team
- subscribe and unsubscribe from apps - see [developer connection](/15.collaboration/5.developer-connection.md#app-subscription-through-teams)
- delete, create, change payment methods connected to the team
- manage all other member roles
- invite new member for any role to the team
- leave the team (if other members are present)
- delete the team

## Limited

- delete, configure & scale chosen apps of the team
- unsubscribe from team apps
- request access on team apps - see [developer connection](/15.collaboration/5.developer-connection.md#app-subscription-through-teams)
- create new apps on behalf of the team
- access code on chosen apps
- leave the team
