fortrabbit gives you a working PHP host out of the box, but most projects need more around it: a Git provider for deployments, an editor that talks to your environment over SSH, a transactional mail service, an APM tool, or a way to keep an eye on uptime.

The pages below collect the integrations and combinations we test most often, grouped by what they do. Pick the ones you need and skip the rest.
