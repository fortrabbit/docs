## About Plausible

[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io) is a privacy-friendly, open-source web analytics tool that doesn't use cookies, complies with GDPR and CCPA without requiring consent banners, and is available as a hosted service with free trials and paid plans or as a self-hosted open-source version at no cost.
