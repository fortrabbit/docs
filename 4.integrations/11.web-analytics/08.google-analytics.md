## About Google Analytics

[Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com) is a web analytics service from Google. The current version, Google Analytics 4 (GA4), uses an event-based data model.

## Privacy considerations

Google Analytics collects user data and uses cookies. In many jurisdictions, this requires explicit user consent through cookie consent banners. Privacy-focused alternatives like Fathom or Plausible can help if GDPR compliance without consent banners is a priority. Review Google's data processing terms and configure Analytics to comply with applicable privacy laws in the relevant region.

## Pricing

Google Analytics is free for most websites. There is a paid version called Google Analytics 360 designed for enterprise customers with advanced needs.
