## About Matomo

[Matomo](https://matomo.org) is an open-source analytics platform available as both a self-hosted solution and a managed cloud service, designed to comply with privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA while avoiding reliance on third-party data collection systems.

Matomo is well known in the PHP scene and can be self-hosted on fortrabbit services.
