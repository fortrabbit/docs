## Email account provider types

There are roughly two paths to host email accounts with your own domain:

- Classical web-hosting packages often include domain registration and email services. Some [domain providers](/4.integrations/02.dns/01.intro.md) also offer email accounts.
- A dedicated email service may have advanced features such as office integration, team management, a more sophisticated mail client, and backups.

## Considerations

- Privacy and security
- GEO location
- Pricing

## Some email account providers

Here are some IMAP/POP3 email account providers popular with our clients:

- [Google Workspace](https://workspace.google.com/) - Full-featured office suite with Gmail for your domain.
- [Namecheap Email](https://www.namecheap.com/hosting/email.aspx) - Email offering from domain provider.
- [ProtonMail](https://protonmail.com) - Secure mail from Switzerland.
- [FastMail](https://www.fastmail.com) - Original email as a service.
- [Zoho Mail](https://www.zoho.com/mail) - Office solution with free entry option.
- [AWS WorkMail](https://aws.amazon.com/workmail) - AWS email and calendar service.
- [Runbox](https://runbox.com) - Secure mail from Norway.

We have no business relation with any of the above mentioned providers. These are not affiliate links.
