## About email accounts

fortrabbit does not provide email hosting or SMTP services. Two distinct use cases require external providers:

1. **Personal email accounts** (IMAP/POP3) — this article
2. **Transactional emails** sent by your application — see [transaction mails](/4.integrations/10.transactional-mail/01.intro.md)

## Integration with fortrabbit

Email account providers operate independently from fortrabbit. Configure your domain's MX (DNS) records to point to your chosen email provider's mail servers; fortrabbit's hosting infrastructure is not involved in email routing.
