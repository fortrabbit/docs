[Ubicloud](https://www.ubicloud.com/docs/github-actions-integration/quickstart) provides hosted runners with Linux and ARM options and a pricing model that is often notably lower than GitHub-hosted runners. The wider platform is open source, which may matter to teams that care about transparency or want a closer look at the underlying approach. This is a practical option when cost is the main decision driver and you still want a service that behaves like hosted infrastructure.
