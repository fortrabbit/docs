[Blacksmith](https://blacksmith.sh/) focuses on being easy to adopt while improving performance and cost compared with GitHub-hosted runners. Its notable strengths include a migration wizard, high-speed caching, ARM support, and additional workflow statistics.

It is worth considering when teams want better CI performance but still prefer a managed service with minimal workflow changes.
