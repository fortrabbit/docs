It's made to deploy and deliver modern PHP websites and web applications, specifically [Craft CMS](/2.craft/1.setup.md), [Laravel](/4.guides/4.laravel.md), [Statamic](/3.statamic/1.setup.md), [October CMS](/4.guides/6.october.md) and [WordPress](/4.guides/5.wordpress.md). The [GitHub integration](/6.deployment/3.github-app.md) provides deployment directly from the repo. Projects are represented as [apps](/13.objects/1.app.md) with [environments](/13.objects/2.app-environment.md). The [collaboration features](/11.collaboration/1.intro.md) map real world relations between developers and stakeholders.

fortrabbit is around [since 2012](https://www.fortrabbit.com/about-us).
