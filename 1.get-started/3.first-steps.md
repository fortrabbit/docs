## 1. Sign up

Create a free personal account over at our dashboard. We won't spam you or sell your data.

## 2. Create a free trial app

After boarding the dashboard, you will be guided to create your first app. Choose the free trial for a quick test drive.

## 3. Tinker and explore

- Deploy some code
  - either with our GitHub integration (git push to deploy)
  - or by SSH, rsync
  - or even classical SFTP
- Explore the dashboard
  - check the environment settings
- Scan our docs here
  - CMS and framework guides
  - collaboration features
  - working with environments
- Contact support if you are stuck anywhere or if you have feedback

## 4. Start for real

- migrate existing projects
- or start new projects here
- invite other developers or your clients
