You enjoy modern PHP development with Laravel, Craft CMS, Symfony or other frameworks and CMS systems? Keep reading.

## Recommended skills

fortrabbit is aimed for professional web developers making a living of turning coffee into code. We anticipate you know about:

- Terminal command line
- Modern PHP and JS
- Git, Composer
- Framework / CMS concepts

Not there yet? Give it a try anyways. fortrabbit is also a path to level up your skills. Our extensive help guides have helped many web designer to become better web developers along the way.

## Collaboration workflows

Web hosting is also about professional relationships. Wether you are a one man army or a team of developers, the fortrabbit dashboard collaboration features can help mapping

- Digital agencies
- Freelancing developers
- Startups
- Remote teams
- Any kind of small and medium sized organizations
