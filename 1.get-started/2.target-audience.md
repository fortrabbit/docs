## Recommended skills

fortrabbit is aimed for professional web developers making a living of turning coffee into code. You know about:

- Terminal command line usage
- Modern PHP and JS
- Git, Composer
- Framework / CMS concepts

Not there yet? Give it a try anyways. fortrabbit is also a path to level up your skills. Our extensive guides have helped many web designers to become better web developers along the way.

## Collaboration workflows

Web hosting is also about professional relationships. Wether you are a one man army or a team of developers, the fortrabbit [collaboration features](/14.concepts/1.collaboration.md) map real world relationships for:

- Digital agencies
- Freelancing developers
- Startups
- Remote teams
- Any kind of small and medium sized organizations
